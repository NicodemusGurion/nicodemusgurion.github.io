> <span id="v1">1</span> Rivalry in worldly increase distracteth you

> <span id="v2">2</span> Until you come to the graves.

> <span id="v3">3</span> Nay, but you will come to know!

> <span id="v4">4</span> Nay, but you will come to know!

> <span id="v5">5</span> Nay, would that you knew (now) with a sure knowledge!

> <span id="v6">6</span> For you will behold hell-fire.

> <span id="v7">7</span> Aye, you will behold it with sure vision.

> <span id="v8">8</span> Then, on that day, you will be asked concerning pleasure.
