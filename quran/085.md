> <span id="v1">1</span> By the heaven, holding mansions of the stars,

> <span id="v2">2</span> And by the Promised Day.

> <span id="v3">3</span> And by the witness and that whereunto he beareth testimony,

> <span id="v4">4</span> (Self-)destroyed were the owners of the ditch

> <span id="v5">5</span> Of the fuel-fed fire,

> <span id="v6">6</span> When they sat by it,

> <span id="v7">7</span> And were themselves the witnesses of what they did to the believers.

> <span id="v8">8</span> They had nothing against them save that they believed in Allah, the Mighty, the Owner of Praise,

> <span id="v9">9</span> Him unto Whom belongeth the Sovereignty of the heavens and the earth; and Allah is of all things the Witness.

> <span id="v10">10</span> See! they who persecute believing men and believing women and repent not, theirs verily will be the doom of hell, and theirs the doom of burning.

> <span id="v11">11</span> See! those who believe and do good works, theirs will be Gardens underneath which rivers flow. That is the Great Success.

> <span id="v12">12</span> See! the punishment of your Lord is stern.

> <span id="v13">13</span> See! He it is Who produceth, then reproduceth,

> <span id="v14">14</span> And He is the Forgiving, the Loving,

> <span id="v15">15</span> Lord of the Throne of Glory,

> <span id="v16">16</span> Doer of what He will.

> <span id="v17">17</span> Hath there come unto you the story of the hosts

> <span id="v18">18</span> Of Pharaoh and (the tribe of) Thamud ?

> <span id="v19">19</span> No, but those who disbelieve live in denial

> <span id="v20">20</span> And Allah, all unseen, surroundeth them.

> <span id="v21">21</span> No, but it is a glorious Qur'an.

> <span id="v22">22</span> On a guarded tablet.
