> <span id="v1">1</span> Say: O disbelievers!

> <span id="v2">2</span> I worship not that which you worship;

> <span id="v3">3</span> Nor worship you that which I worship.

> <span id="v4">4</span> And I shall not worship that which you worship.

> <span id="v5">5</span> Nor will you worship that which I worship.

> <span id="v6">6</span> Unto you your religion, and unto me my religion.
