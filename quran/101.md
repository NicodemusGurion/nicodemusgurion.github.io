> <span id="v1">1</span> The Calamity!

> <span id="v2">2</span> What is the Calamity ?

> <span id="v3">3</span> Ah, what will convey unto thee what the Calamity is!

> <span id="v4">4</span> A day wherein mankind will be as thickly-scattered moths

> <span id="v5">5</span> And the mountains will become as carded wool.

> <span id="v6">6</span> Then, as for him whose scales are heavy (with good works),

> <span id="v7">7</span> He will live a pleasant life.

> <span id="v8">8</span> But as for him whose scales are light,

> <span id="v9">9</span> A bereft and Hungry One will be his mother,

> <span id="v10">10</span> Ah, what will convey unto thee what she is! -

> <span id="v11">11</span> Raging Fire.
