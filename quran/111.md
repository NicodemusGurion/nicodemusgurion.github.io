> <span id="v1">1</span> The power of Abu Lahab will perish, and he will perish.

> <span id="v2">2</span> His wealth and gains will not exempt him.

> <span id="v3">3</span> He will be plunged in flaming Fire,

> <span id="v4">4</span> And his wife, the wood-carrier,

> <span id="v5">5</span> Will have upon her neck a halter of palm-fibre.
