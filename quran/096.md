> <span id="v1">1</span> Read: In the name of your Lord Who created,

> <span id="v2">2</span> Created man from a clot.

((96:2, Creation - Human, Blood clot, Humans created from clot))

> <span id="v3">3</span> Read: And your Lord is the Most Bounteous,

> <span id="v4">4</span> Who teaches by the pen,

> <span id="v5">5</span> Teaches man that which he knew not.

> <span id="v6">6</span> No, but verily man is rebellious

> <span id="v7">7</span> That he thinks himself independent!

> <span id="v8">8</span> See! unto your Lord is the return.

> <span id="v9">9</span> Have you seen him who dissuades

> <span id="v10">10</span> A slave when he prays ?

> <span id="v11">11</span> Have you seen if he relies on the guidance (of Allah)

> <span id="v12">12</span> Or encourages piety ?

> <span id="v13">13</span> Have you seen if he denies (Allah's guidance) and is contrary ?

> <span id="v14">14</span> Is he then unaware that Allah seeth ?

> <span id="v15">15</span> No, but if he cease not We will seize him by the forelock -

> <span id="v16">16</span> The lying, sinful forelock -

> <span id="v17">17</span> Then let him call upon his henchmen!

> <span id="v18">18</span> We will call the guards of hell.

> <span id="v19">19</span> No, Obey not you him. But prostrate thyself, and draw near (unto Allah).
