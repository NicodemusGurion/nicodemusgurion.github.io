> <span id="v1">1</span> Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind,

> <span id="v2">2</span> The King of mankind,

> <span id="v3">3</span> The God of mankind,

> <span id="v4">4</span> From the evil of the sneaking whisperer,

> <span id="v5">5</span> Who whispereth in the hearts of mankind,

> <span id="v6">6</span> Of the jinn and of mankind.
