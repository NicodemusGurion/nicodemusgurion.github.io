> <span id="v1">1</span> Rivalry in worldly increase distracteth you

> <span id="v2">2</span> Until ye come to the graves.

> <span id="v3">3</span> Nay, but ye will come to know!

> <span id="v4">4</span> Nay, but ye will come to know!

> <span id="v5">5</span> Nay, would that ye knew (now) with a sure knowledge!

> <span id="v6">6</span> For ye will behold hell-fire.

> <span id="v7">7</span> Aye, ye will behold it with sure vision.

> <span id="v8">8</span> Then, on that day, ye will be asked concerning pleasure.
